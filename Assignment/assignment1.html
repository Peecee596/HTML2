ABOUT SWATHI SUNSOURCE

Swathi Sunsource Power Private Ltd. (SSP), a seed sowed & reaped by proficient professionals to help meet the rising demand for cleaner energy in India and the world. SSP was founded with an endeavour to render solutions for a greener and cleaner future. We aim to be a leading player in the Energy segment, producing and providing comprehensive Renewable Energy Solutions with the necessary versatility, durability and energy security to our clientele. Our main focus is on Solar Energy driven solutions which are efficient and effective. Driven by conviction, initiative & dynamism, we have oriented all our efforts to deliver qualitative & easy maintenance products for multifarious use not only pan India but globally too.
