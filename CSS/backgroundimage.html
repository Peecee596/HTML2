Reliance launches JioFiber postpaid internet connection starting at Rs 399 per month

Reliance Jio has announced its new postpaid JioFiber plans along with 4K set top box starting a monthly subscription of Rs 399. The JioFiber postpaid plan is claimed to have no “zero upfront entry cost” and there are no security deposit or installation charges. Reliance Jio is offering postpaid plans starting at Rs 399 per month with the option to pay annually and half-yearly. Also, it is offering a 4K Set Top Box at no extra cost. But subscribers will have to pay Rs 1,000 as a refundable security deposit. This set top box can be used playing content from OTT apps. For plans of Rs 999 and above, subscribers will get access to up to 15 paid OTT apps.
